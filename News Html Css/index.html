SEA Games 2023: Philippines assured of silver in Wild Rift esports event

MANILA, Philippines—The Philippines’ Sibol Wild Rift advanced to the gold medal match of the League of Legends: Wild Rift esports event in the 32nd Southeast Asian Games at Phnom Penh, Cambodia on Sunday.

Sibol defeated Vietnam, 3-2, for a chance to bring home either a silver or a gold medal for the Philippines.

Golden Heart Dajao willed the Philippines to a come-from-behind victory with 72% kill participation.

Whichever country the Philippines will face in the Finals has yet to be determined.
